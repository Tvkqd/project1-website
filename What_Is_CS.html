What is Computer Science?

Computer Science is the study of computers including hardware, software, and how to interact with human.

Computer Scientists solve problems using technology. Our daily life nowaday is surrounded almost by computers, from the phone in your pocket to the car that you drive everyday, from the wifi at home to the network system at workplace, from the traffic light on the road to credit machines at the stores.

Computer Scientists are there to solve the puzzels and make life become more convenient.
