Why Computer Science?

The field of Computer Science is one that has had a ton of growth. This means that almost every company has a use for a Computer Scientist, and by extension, there are tons of jobs. Not to mention that there aren't enough Computer Scientists around, so the pay is higher than most other jobs within said company of the same level.

Pay and job security isn't everything, however. There are tons of other factors that make Computer Science a great degree to pursue. For example, since the demand is so high, the benefits received will likely also be bountiful. The job locations are also usually based in urban areas, so travel to events will be significantly shorter.
