Best Entry-Level Jobs

As mentioned before, the field of Computer Science is meta and in-demand because of its versatility in what you you can do with it.

Below are some of the best ENTRY-LEVEL jobs you can acquire with a fresh Computer Science degree and a few quick certifications according to indeed.com just last year:


National Average Salary: $120,728

Cloud Engineers manage and maintain a company's cloud-based computer systems. Their range of duties range from installing and updating cloud-based software onto the machines to maintaining security of the company cloud.

Cloud Engineer - Image from https://www.udacity.com/blog/2021/01/cloud-engineer-jobs-what-you-need-to-know-for-2021.html



National Average Salary: $115,006

Software Developers create and maintain new software for specific end users. The platform they use and type of apps they make varies greatly based on what kind of software is being create, and they communicate often with the other departments involved in their work to know what requirements need to be met

Software Developer - Image from https://www.topresume.com/career-advice/senior-software-developer-job-description



National Average Salary: $86,975

Game Developers are Computer Science professionals that use their skills to create video games which are played by the end-users. They develop games for different consoles and platforms and frequently test the game for errors or bugs.

Game Developer - Image from https://theartcareerproject.com/how-to-become-a-video-game-designer/



National Average Salary: $113,821

Data Scientists are all about analyzing data and reformatting it into a more usable and efficient form. They spend a lot of their time researching and developing new data models based on new technology trends.

Data Scientist - Image from https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=X3paOmcrTjQ



National Average Salary: $95,672

Digitial Designers are the creative side of Computer Science. They create and develop graphic designs for all different kinds of computer software and websites. Their work is flexible as they can work full-time, part-time, or freelance for commissions as a solo agent.

Digital Designer - Image from https://careerfoundry.com/en/blog/ui-design/digital-design-guide/
